About Me

My name is Greg Beas, and I am a front-end developer. Originally of a mechanical engineering background, I enjoy learning about and using the tools available to me to build new and exciting projects. My love for web development began when I realized the projects I can create don't have to be physical, and the tools I learn about don't have to be ones I can hold in my hand.

Now, having transitioned exclusively to digital tools, I am committed to building accessible and responsive digital products, and passionate about expanding my knowledge on the technologies available to do so.

Some tools I've been using:

  • JavaScript (ES6+)
  • React
  • Vite
  • TypeScript
  • Node.js
  • Sass
  • Tailwind
  • Bootstrap
  • Figma
